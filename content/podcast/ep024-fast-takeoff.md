In this episode, we break down the three converging forces driving rapid AI adoption in healthcare: model capability crossing clinical thresholds, infrastructure readiness under HIPAA, and acute economic pressure from staffing and margin compression.

We discuss why the current moment is fundamentally different from previous waves of health IT "transformation" and what it means for oncology practices trying to navigate the landscape.
