## Why Oncology Practices Should Care More Than Anyone

Oncology EHR workflows aren't like primary care. They're not even like cardiology or orthopedics. They involve:

1. **Complex medication regimens** (chemo, immunotherapy, targeted therapy combinations)
2. **Toxicities management** (CTCAE grading, dose adjustments, supportive care)
3. **Clinical trial integration** (protocol-specific documentation, eligibility checks)
4. **Molecular testing workflows** (NGS results integration, biomarker-driven pathways)
5. **Survivorship care plans** (long-term follow-up, late effects monitoring)

Each of these requires **clinical informatics expertise** — the exact expertise Oracle just eliminated. The Cerner specialists who understood how to configure chemotherapy order sets for dose-banding? Gone. The engineers who built oncology-specific clinical trial modules? Gone. The analysts who optimized tumor board workflows? Gone.

This creates a $500M support gap that Epic can't fill fast enough — and that AI infrastructure won't solve for years.

## The Contrarian Take: This Isn't About AI vs. Humans
